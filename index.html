Buying Process & Decision Factors

Key Buying Factors

Compliance Assurance

Solution must ensure regulatory readiness (FDA, EASA, ISO).

Proven Industry Expertise

Vendor must show deep knowledge of regulated industries.

Integration Capability

Seamless connection with existing ERP/QMS is a must.

Measurable ROI

Clear financial impact must be demonstrated within 12-18 months.

Decision Process

Sarah leads a committee-based evaluation involving Quality, Supply Chain, IT, and Finance teams. The process typically spans 6-12 months and includes formal RFP/RFI procedures with pilot program requirements to validate solution effectiveness.

Common Objections to Address

Budget Concerns

Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) benefits need to be clearly shown.

Security & Compliance

Must prove cloud solutions meet strict data security standards.

Change Disruption

Fear of disrupting established workflows and supplier relationships.

Managed Service Skepticism

Uncertainty about relying on external teams for critical functions.

IT Integration

IT department resistance to new platform integration and support.
